About Us

Brooklyn Birdhouse is a brand new start-up company providing top quality entertainment & creative services. We specialize in event/party bands, photography, videography, and logo design services! We are a one-stop-shop vendor! We are not an "agency", we are a group of young creative and musically talented individuals who have developed close bonds over our college years and are ready to provide something fresh, new, and unique while keeping our services affordable.

Entertainment

You bring your friends, family, and loved ones to the dancefloor and we’ll take care of the rest! The Brooklyn Birdhouse Band is a tight-knit group of talented musicians and friends who understand intimacy, chemistry, and how to enhance good vibes with good sounds.


As professional musicians graduating from Berklee College of Music and the Boston Conservatory, we’ve performed with various big-name artists and Grammy Award-Winning artists as well as touring worldwide. We can incorporate all styles of music into our set including (but not limited to): jazz, rock, pop, R&B, and funk.


Creative

Visualize your special moment, generate a mental picture, fantastic!- now if you don’t mind, we’ll go ahead a capture that with our cameras. Whether you want still or moving images, we’ve got it all. Our photography and videography services that we provide on the day of your event are further polished with high-end digital editing and post processing at our in-house Brooklyn Birdhouse Studio; fully equiped with audio and visual resources.


All photos a videos will be delivered digitally to you in a thumbdrive right to your doorstep (with the help of your neighborhood postman).
